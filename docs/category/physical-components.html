Physical Components

📄️ General Node Arcitecture

Each ACPS node features sensors, actuators, and a microcontroller to execute specific tasks and communicate data through IoT for effective monitoring and control.

📄️ Node 1: Access Control System

Automates secure entry management by authorizing or denying access based on RFID scans.

📄️ Node 2: Contactless Thermometer

Provides non-invasive, real-time temperature readings for health monitoring in public and medical settings.

📄️ Node 3: Energy Management System

Monitors and controls energy consumption to optimize efficiency and reduce operational costs in buildings.

📄️ Node 4: Fire and Smoke Detector

Detects and alerts the presence of fire and smoke, enhancing safety protocols in residential and commercial spaces.

📄️ Node 5: Light Intensity, Loud Noise and Color Detector

Monitors environmental light, sound, and color, adjusting system responses for optimal ambiance control.

📄️ Node 6: Local Weather Station

Collects and displays local weather data, aiding in community weather tracking and environmental education.

📄️ Node 7: Motion Detector

Enhances security by detecting and alerting to motion within its vicinity, suitable for homes and commercial spaces.

📄️ Node 8: Obstacle Detector

Employs ultrasonic sensing to detect and alert to obstacles, critical for robotic and autonomous vehicle navigation.

📄️ Node 9: Orientation Visualizer, Free Fall, and Crash Detector

Tracks device orientation and detects falls or crashes, important for automotive and mobile device safety.

📄️ Node 10: Temperature Control System

Manages and regulates environmental temperatures to maintain specific conditions in sensitive areas.

📄️ Node 11: Vibration and Shock Detector

Monitors vibrations and shocks to foresee and prevent potential damage in industrial settings.

📄️ Node 12: Voice-Based Assistant

Facilitates voice-activated control of devices, enhancing user interaction and accessibility in smart environments.
